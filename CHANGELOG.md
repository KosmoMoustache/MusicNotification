### Added

## [1.6.0] - 2024-01-27

### Added

- Allow mods/resource pack to provide their own musics.json #21
- Album Cover are now retrieve from musics.json

- Use the new sprite system
- Renamed settings MUTE_MOD to MUTE_SELF

### Fixed

## [1.5.1] - 2023-12-14

### Fixed
